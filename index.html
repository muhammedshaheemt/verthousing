Empowering Living for Adults with Autism and Learning Disabilities We are a non-profit provider of supported housing, dedicated to helping adults with autism, learning disabilities, and mental health issues live whole and independent lives. In partnership with local authorities, we provide high-quality housing and specialised care, creating a safe and supportive environment where everyone can thrive. Our homes are more than just a place to live; they are a community. We believe in empowering our residents, offering the support they need while respecting their independence."

About Vert Housing

Established in 2020, Vert Housing is a dedicated non-profit organisation committed to providing exceptional supported housing solutions across the UK.

We specialise in working with local authorities and specialist care providers to support individuals with various needs, with a primary focus on adults with autism spectrum disorder.

Our mission is to collaborate with the finest landlords and support providers, ensuring we deliver the highest quality housing and care services to our tenants.

Quality Homes Designed for You

At Vert Housing, we believe a great home is the foundation for a great life. That's why we've designed and adapted our properties to meet the unique needs of adults with autism and other support requirements.

Our homes are more than just accessible; they are places where comfort and safety come first, all while promoting independence and community connections. We're committed to providing the highest standards of living, so our residents can feel secure, supported, and truly at home.
